Our story

We started in 2011 looking to fill a need for a data driven approach to design. It wasn’t long before we received recognition for our work and, what began as an idyllic start-up, transformed into a feasible business and then into the working information design agency that we are today.
Today, within the constantly evolving context of data and the digital environment, we are continuously excited by the new challenges and new technologies to craft high impact and innovative visual experiences.

What we believe in

Quality is our end goal. We’ve been able to build strong relationships and long-term collaborations with our clients because, for each and every project, our aim is to craft the highest quality end result.

Clients as partners. Extraordinary relationships help us deliver extraordinary results.
That is why we make sure our clients form a key part of each process, making them one more member of our team. Only by working with them and not for them, we’re able to craft visual narratives that perfectly reflect core messaging.

Value-added products. We see value in building forward-thinking tools and resources for our clients to create their own data-driven visuals when they want and using their most up-to-date metrics.

Long-term vision. We’re always aiming to build products that last, not only by utilizing future proof technologies, but also by embracing each client’s key messaging with forward-thinking strategy and narratives.

The Elkano way

Our projects are built around a systematic approach of clearly defined processes that are proven to produce consistently high-quality results. For each project, we have small, dedicated teams that look to collaborate with our clients from beginning to end to create impactful, value-added results.
Through specialized data-driven design, each project is a journey as our team transforms complex information into visually engaging digital experiences, products and content.
